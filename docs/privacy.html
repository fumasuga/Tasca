Privacy Policy

Tasca Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Tasca (the "App") values the protection of users' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect and how we use and protect it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (stored encrypted)
  • Task Information: Created tasks, completed tasks, task content, outputs, URL links
  • Usage Information: App usage, error information
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, unique device identifier

3. Purpose of Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the App
  • Managing and authenticating user accounts
  • Storing and syncing task data
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Ensuring security and preventing unauthorized use
  • Analyzing for service improvement

4. Data Storage and Security

  1. We store data using Supabase (PostgreSQL database).
  2. All data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS), and users can only access their own data.
  3. Passwords are stored encrypted (hashed) and are never stored in plain text.
  4. All communication with the database is encrypted using HTTPS (SSL/TLS).

5. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not provide users' personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • When the user has consented
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect human life, body, or property
  • When particularly necessary for public health or the sound development of children

6. Data Retention

User data is retained until the account is deleted. When an account is deleted, all related data is also deleted.

7. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Right to access their personal information
  • Right to request correction or deletion of their personal information
  • Right to request account deletion
  • Right to request data export

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact form.

8. Cookies

The App uses session information to maintain authentication status but does not use cookies for tracking purposes.

9. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services (Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy)

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy as necessary. When changes are made, we will post them on this page and notify users of significant changes.

12. Contact

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact form.
