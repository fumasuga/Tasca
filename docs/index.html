About the App

Tasca is a simple and beautiful task management app. It helps you efficiently manage your daily tasks and record your achievements to improve your productivity.

Visualize your achievements with GitHub-style contribution graphs, and add memos and links to each task to record your learnings and accomplishments.

Key Features

  • Simple Task Management - Intuitively add, complete, and delete tasks
  • GitHub-Style Contribution Graph - Visually display your completion history for the past year to boost motivation
  • Output Feature - Record memos and reflections for each task to build your knowledge base
  • URL Link Feature - Save URLs related to your tasks and easily manage reference materials
  • Completion History Management - Organize and display completed tasks by date
  • Multi-Language Support - Supports English, Japanese, French, and Korean
  • Beautiful UI Design - Modern design and smooth animations provide an excellent user experience
  • Dark Mode Support - Eye-friendly dark mode for comfortable use at night
  • Secure Authentication - Secure authentication system powered by Supabase protects your data

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is the app free to use?

A. Yes, Tasca is completely free to use. There are no additional charges or subscriptions.

Q. Where is my data stored?

A. All data is securely stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database). Row Level Security (RLS) ensures that users can only access their own data.

Q. Can I use the app offline?

A. The current version requires an internet connection. We are considering adding offline functionality in the future.

Q. Can I export my data?

A. The current version does not have data export functionality, but we are considering adding it in the future.

Q. How do I delete my account?

A. Account deletion functionality is currently being implemented. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us.

Q. Can I sync across multiple devices?

A. Yes, if you log in with the same account, your data will automatically sync across multiple devices.

Q. How do I report a bug?

A. If you encounter any bugs or have feedback, please report them through the GitHub Issues page or the App Store review section.
